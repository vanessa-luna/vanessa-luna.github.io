DIY 1: Buying the materials

diy ~ 2019-03-15

This post lists the EXACT materials I use and where to buy them.

IMPORTANT - I do not explain WHY to use these materials and why others do not work in this post. Please refer to the youtube video or this post: DIY: Variations

Like video? Walk through the store with me in this video: A trip to Joann Fabrics

REQUIRED

The MOST important things are the fabric and elastic. These must not be substituted.

A LOT I KNOW. However, with all this, you will be able to make a LOT of them. 1 yard of fabric will give you approziametly 20 or more. The wasit band elastic will give you about 3-5. The comfort elastic will give you about 10-15. The hook and eyes will give you plenty.

To make it cheaper:

  1. Buy from the remnants section! Only buy what you need for a couple to try and fail on before committing to a whole yard
  2. Maybe you can buy the waist band elastic by the yard so only buy your hips measurement + 6 inches rounded up to the nearest yard.
  3. If you already have a pair of scissors Don't buy them!
  4. This could reduce your starting cost to around $30.

You of course can use any old cutting board you might already have.

Home materials you might already have

Comfort Elastic I would not trust at-home elastics for this
Ring and Sliders maybe a bar, but these sliders and rings tend to be too small
Hook and Eye Old bra is great for these
Scissors You likely have some that will do, but ideally the sharper the better
Hand Sewing needle and thread Lots of us have something like this lying around.
