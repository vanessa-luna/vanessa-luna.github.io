A failed glue experiment: Tear Mender

experiments ~ 2017-11-16

I tried using a glue called Tear Mender in attempt to glue the elastic to the fabric before sewing it. Mistake.

It did not work the way I hoped. I seeped deep into the fabric and wouldn't set. Then it pilled and didn't look good. So, not this kind of glue.

I know, exciting post...

I wish I had more help.
