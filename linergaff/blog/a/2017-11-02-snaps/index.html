Experiment using snaps instead of hook and eyes for the elastic

experiments ~ 2017-11-02

Today, a friend had the materials for putting snaps on stuff so I tried making a gaff with snaps!

I was surprised. It went well, looked good, and functioned fine. For at least two hours of sitting and standing everything was fine.

I am definitely going to get some people to try this out. Especially those who have tried the hook and eye style already.
