New drawers for Vanessa!

vandwelling ~ 2017-09-28

My wonderful friend helped me install new drawers in Vanessa. Totally maximizing some of the space in the rear beneath my bed. I am super excited about them.

The left side, which is empty for now, will hold a removable plastic drawer storage solution. This is so I can continue to remove my long board if I ever use it again. If I didn't have that pesky thing I might have filled the entire rear with drawers. Anyway.

Design

These are basic construction. The drawers are 4 panels glued and screwed together on the sides. The drawer base is inset into the wood 1/8 of an inch.

The frame is to walls with two small back plates to stop the drawers.

I reused drawer slides from an old ikea dresser I had. The slides are mounted onto the side walls and inset into the drawers as deep as they can go.
