Magcraft Pricing Table

vandwelling ~ 2017-03-21

This is the table I put together when researching magnets. I wanted to find the best deals for the right magnets. The cost per was a crucial element to that.

View my post about magnets in my van here

Notes

I suggest purchasing the magnets through Home Depot as they offer free shipping over $45 and free shipping to stores for in store pickup. Search for the id number and you'll find it easily.

Magnets @ HomeDepot

Also, this company sells other types of magnets but I didn't imagine them as useful so I did not include them.

Legend

$ = I bought this
! = I will likely buy this
+ = Seems worth buying

All measurements are in inches.

Disc Magnets

Best price and power with most variety. None of the discs are over 1 inch diameter which means they are no bigger than a US Quarter.

The onces I purchased here are used to hold up my insulation panels, valence, hang drying towels, hold tools, my key, parts of my shoe rack storage. Seem to be the best one on here for price, quantity and strength.

Ring Magnets

I have not purchased any of these. However they may be useful since they have a hole in them. You can tie things to it, or run a rod through it, which may have applications for you. The pull force is weaker than disc magnet equivalents. The one disc magnet that matches outer diameter and thickness has double the pull force. NSN0814 vs NSN0802
